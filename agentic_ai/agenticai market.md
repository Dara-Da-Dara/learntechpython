# ISG – Information Services Group  
## Agentic AI Platforms Buyers Guide (2025)

## 1. What is ISG?

**ISG** stands for **Information Services Group**.

ISG is a **global technology research, advisory, and consulting firm** that provides independent, data-driven insights into enterprise technology markets. It is widely recognized for its **ISG Buyers Guides**, which evaluate and compare software platforms across domains such as **AI, automation, cloud, analytics, and agentic AI**.
