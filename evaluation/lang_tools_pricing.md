# LangGraph, Langfuse, and LangSmith: Free vs Paid

## LangGraph

### ✅ Free
- Core LangGraph library is open source (MIT license) and free to use.
- Self-hosted deployments are free aside from infrastructure costs.

### ⚠️ Paid / Platform Usage
- Hosted LangGraph API/Deployment (LangSmith Deployment) has a free tier but paid plans for scaling beyond limits.

**Summary:** Free to use core/library; hosted API may require paid plan for heavy usage.

---

## Langfuse

### ✅ Free Options
- Free cloud tier for hobby/POC usage (~50k units/month).
- Fully open-source and self-hostable for free.

### ⚠️ Paid Options
- Paid cloud plans for higher quotas or enterprise usage.

**Summary:** Free for self-hosting or small usage; paid for higher limits.

---

## LangSmith

### ✅ Free Tier
- Free developer plan with 1 seat and ~5,000 traces/month.

### ⚠️ Paid Plans
- Plus plan: ~$39/user/month, higher trace quota.
- Additional traces billed per usage beyond free tier.
- Enterprise plan: custom pricing with advanced features.

**Summary:** Free starter tier available; paid plans needed for teams or heavy usage.

---

## Quick Comparison

| Feature | Langfuse | LangSmith |
|---------|----------|-----------|
| Free Tier (Hosted) | Yes (~50k units/month) | Yes (5k traces/month) |
| Self-Hosted Free | Yes (fully OSS) | Limited / Enterprise only |
| Paid Options | Yes (cloud limits, enterprise) | Yes (Plus, extra traces) |
| Best for Cost-Free Use | ✔︎ (self-hosted) | ✔︎ (starter tier only) |

---

## Bottom Line
- **Langfuse:** Truly free and open-source, excellent for self-hosting.
- **LangSmith:** Free starter tier, but paid plans required for higher usage.
